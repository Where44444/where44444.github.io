Privacy Policy:
1. Information We Collect
-Location Data

Information you provide us directly:
-Email Address
-Password
-Various Business Data

2. Information Usage
-We don't view your information for any purpose unless you give permission
-Your location data will be used to enable your on/off site status being visible
  to supervisors within your company, even when the app is closed or not in use.
  Location data will only be collected while your shift is active. Supervisors
  will not see any specific location data other than that you are "On Site" or
  "Off Site"
-No location data is collected when you aren't supposed to be working a shift
-All your other information won't be used for any purpose

3. Data Collection Methods
-Location info is collected through your mobile device's GPS/Network location API
  as latitude longitude coordinates every 60 seconds only during the time you
  are assigned to be working a shift at any location. Your device then uses the
  coordinates to calculate your on/off site status. Your status (true/false value,
  no coordinates) is transmitted securely over HTTPS to a Google Firebase server,
  your coordinates never leave your device. Your status is retrieved by a device
  that belongs to a supervisor within your company, and displayed as your status
  being "On Site" or "Off Site".
-All other info you provide to us is provided through the app
