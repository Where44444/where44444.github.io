Privacy Policy:
1. Information We Collect

Information you provide us directly:
-The name of your business
-The address of your business
-The business phone number
-The business email address
-The emergency phone number for your area
-Any additional comments you want emergency services to see

Information we gather:
-The name of the employee logged in
-The ID of the employee logged in

2. Information Usage
-Your information won't be used for any purpose except to send to emergency services when needed.
-The employee ID is only used to verify the identity of the employee that logs in to the app.

3. Data Collection Methods
-All info you provide to us is provided through a form in the app that a manager or admin fills out.
-Employee ID's and names are provided to us after you give the app permission to access your contacts. They are gathered automatically from the Clover employee list.
